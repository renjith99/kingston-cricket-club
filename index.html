Kingston United Cricket Club

United by Passion, Driven by Teamwork

Welcome to Kingston United Cricket Club — a place where passion for cricket meets community spirit. We are a growing leather-ball cricket team built on respect, discipline, and the belief that every player has a role to play in our success. With the guidance of dedicated coaches and leaders, we focus on skill development, teamwork, and creating opportunities for players to compete at higher levels.
