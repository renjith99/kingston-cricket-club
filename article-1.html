Bangladesh Edges Out Afghanistan in Thrilling T20I Encounter

Published on October 02, 2025

In a captivating T20I held in Sharjah, Bangladesh's nerve held steady as they successfully chased down Afghanistan's total of 151, winning the match by 4 wickets. The victory gives them a crucial 1-0 lead in the series.

Batting first, Afghanistan put up a respectable score of 151 for 9 in their 20 overs. Despite a challenging pitch, their batsmen managed to build partnerships and set a competitive target for the visitors.

In response, Bangladesh's run chase was a calculated affair. They managed the required run rate effectively, and despite losing wickets at key intervals, they reached the target in 18.4 overs, finishing at 153 for 6. The victory was a testament to their batting depth and ability to handle pressure in the closing stages of the game.

The teams will meet again tomorrow for the second T20I.
