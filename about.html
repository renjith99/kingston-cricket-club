About Our Club

Pride in Tradition, Grace in Play.

Building a united cricket family in Kingston — where passion meets discipline, and every player finds their place.

Our History

Kingston United Cricket Club was founded with the vision of bringing passionate cricketers together to form Kingston’s most unified and disciplined leather-ball cricket community.

Our members represent diverse cultures and backgrounds, but we are united by our shared love for the sport and our commitment to nurturing a strong, inclusive cricketing culture.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to develop talent, instill discipline, and offer a structured pathway for cricketers in Kingston. We believe in:

  • Respect: Every individual deserves dignity and fairness.
  • Teamwork: We win together, grow together, and learn together.
  • Excellence: Commitment to improvement in training, fitness, and match performance.
  • Inclusion: Welcoming players of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels.
