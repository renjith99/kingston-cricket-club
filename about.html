    Our History

    Kingston United Cricket Club was founded with a singular vision: to unite passionate cricketers under one banner of discipline, excellence, and inclusivity.

    Our members represent diverse cultures and backgrounds, but on the field, we are one. United by the leather ball, driven by teamwork, and committed to nurturing a professional sporting culture in Kingston.

    Our Core Values

    Respect

    Every individual deserves dignity. We play hard, but we respect our opponents, our officials, and each other.

    Teamwork

    No individual is bigger than the club. We win together, we lose together, and we grow together.

    Excellence

    A relentless commitment to improvement—in training, fitness, strategy, and match-day execution.

    Inclusion

    Cricket is for everyone. We welcome players of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels into our family.
