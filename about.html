About Our Club

Pride in Tradition, Grace in Play.

Building a united cricket family in Kingston, where passion meets discipline and every player finds their place.

Our History

Kingston United Cricket Club was founded with a vision to bring together passionate cricketers in Kingston under one banner. What began as a simple idea — to create a true leather-ball cricket club — has quickly grown into a vibrant community of players committed to discipline, teamwork, and excellence.

From our early beginnings, the club has become a space where players from different backgrounds, regions, and cultures come together, united by their love for the game. Our journey is one of building not just a cricket team, but a cricketing culture in Kingston — one that inspires young players, welcomes experienced cricketers, and creates lasting memories both on and off the field.

Our Mission & Values

At Kingston United, our mission is clear: to nurture talent, build discipline, and create opportunities for cricketers in Kingston to grow and shine. We believe in:

  • Respect: Every player, opponent, and supporter deserves dignity on and off the field.
  • Teamwork: Cricket is a team sport — we win together, we learn together, and we grow together.
  • Excellence: Whether it’s in training, matches, or attitude, we strive to raise our standards every day.
  • Inclusion: Our club welcomes players from all walks of life, reflecting the diversity and unity of Kingston.
