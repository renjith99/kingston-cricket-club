About Our Club

Pride in Tradition, Grace in Play.

Building a united cricket family in Kingston — where passion meets discipline, and every player finds their place.

Our History

Kingston United Cricket Club was founded with a vision to bring passionate players together under one banner. What began as a small idea to build a true leather-ball cricket club has grown into one of Kingston’s most disciplined cricket communities.

Our members come from diverse backgrounds, cultures, and regions — yet the spirit of cricket unites us. We are committed to building a cricketing culture that supports youth development, respects tradition, and creates unforgettable experiences on and off the field.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: to nurture talent, build discipline, and offer a structured pathway for cricketers in Kingston. We believe in:

  • Respect: Every player, opponent, and supporter deserves dignity on and off the field.
  • Teamwork: We win together, learn together, and grow together.
  • Excellence: From training to match days, we strive to raise the bar every single day.
  • Inclusion: Our club reflects Kingston’s diversity and welcomes players of all backgrounds and skill levels.
